Privacy Policy for AL-AQWAL

Last updated: December 2025

1. Information Collection

AL-AQWAL collects minimal personal information only when you voluntarily submit it through our support forms (Email Support, Report Bug, Suggest Feature) or contribution form. This information includes:

  • Name: To address you personally in our responses
  • Email Address: To respond to your inquiries or notify you about your contribution
  • Age: To understand our user demographics (optional)
  • Message/Quote Content: The content of your support request or quote contribution

This information is sent directly to our email address (mharoon12301@gmail.com) for developer communication purposes only. We do not use this data for marketing, advertising, or share it with third parties.

If you do not use these forms, the app operates entirely offline and does not collect any personal information.

2. Data Storage

All quotes and preferences are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties, except when you voluntarily submit information through our support or contribution forms, which is sent directly to our email address.

3. Permissions

The app does not request any storage or media permissions. When you save quotes as images, the app uses the system's Share menu, which allows you to choose where to save the image without requiring any permissions from the app. We do not access, read, or collect any of your personal files or data.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is completely self-contained.

5. Data Security

Any personal information you voluntarily submit through our forms is sent securely via email to our developer account. We do not store this information in any database or third-party service. The information is used solely for responding to your inquiries or processing your contributions.

6. Children's Privacy

If a child under 13 submits information through these forms, we will handle it in accordance with applicable privacy laws. Parents can contact us at mharoon12301@gmail.com if they have concerns about their child's data.

8. Your Consent
